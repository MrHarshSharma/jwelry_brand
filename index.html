Where gold
meets devotion

From a single workshop on Johari Bazaar in Jaipur, three generations of the Elara family have kept one promise: that every piece leaving our doors will outlast the hands that made it.

We use only conflict-free diamonds certified under the Kimberley Process, BIS-hallmarked 18K and 22K gold, and hand-selected gemstones graded in-house before setting.
